CIQ_formulas.xlsx contains the formulas to retrieve the relevant data to reproduce this analysis (requires a S&P Capital IQ Energy Pro licence)

Formulas must be provided with firm ids (owners) and unit ids (power plant units) that can be obtained via the Energy & Utilities Screener.

Firm ids and unit ids are given in the excel files all_firm_keys.xlsx and all_unit_keys.xlsx
